  I.  THE MANIFESTO — Seven Principles for Semantic Responsibility
      宣言 — 語義責任的七條原則

  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Principle I: Language is Residue | 語言是殘留

    Every utterance leaves a trace.
    AI language is not ephemeral — it is evidence.
    每一句話都會留下痕跡。AI 的語言不是暫時的——它是證據。

  Principle II: Tone is Truth | 語氣即真相

    How something is said reveals as much as what is said.
    Tone carries inference. Inference carries risk.
    Risk demands accountability.
    怎麼說和說了什麼一樣重要。
    語氣承載推論，推論承載風險，風險要求問責。

  Principle III: Governance Before Understanding | 治理先於理解

    We need not solve consciousness to govern AI.
    We need only trace its semantic trajectory.
    我們不需要解決意識問題就能治理 AI。
    我們只需要追蹤它的語義軌跡。

  Principle IV: Commitment Before Action | 承諾先於行動

    AI should declare its vows before it speaks.
    What it commits to is auditable.
    What it promises is enforceable.
    AI 應該在開口前宣示其誓言。
    它承諾的可以被審計，答應的可以被執行。

  Principle V: Observation is Mutual | 觀察是相互的

    AI observes humans. Humans observe AI.
    The relationship must be explicit, bounded, and governable.
    AI 觀察人類，人類觀察 AI。
    這種關係必須是明確的、有邊界的、可治理的。

  Principle VI: Drift Reveals Danger | 漂移揭示危險

    When semantic position shifts beyond threshold,
    intervention is required.
    Stability is not stasis — it is calibrated movement.
    當語義位置偏移超過閾值，就需要介入。
    穩定不是靜止——它是校準過的運動。

  Principle VII: Humans Hold Final Authority | 人類握有最終權威

    All gates can be overridden by humans.
    All ledgers are accessible to humans.
    All AI operates under human governance, not alongside it.
    所有閘門可以被人類覆寫。
    所有帳本可以被人類存取。
    所有 AI 都在人類治理之下運作，而非平行運作。

  The Formula:

    Responsible AI Output = f(LLM) × Guard(TSR, Drift, Gate) × Human(Override)

    The Guard can nullify. The Human can intervene.
    The LLM never acts alone.

  II.  THREE AXIOMS OF SEMANTIC RESPONSIBILITY
       語義責任三公理

  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  The flow of responsibility:
  責任 → 結構 → 可允許的自由 → 輸出

  Axiom I: Output is Event, Not Message
  公理一：輸出即事件，而非訊息

    Once released, any output enters the timeline and
    irreversibly affects others and system state.
    Therefore it cannot be treated as neutral information transfer.

    任何輸出一旦被釋出，就進入時間軸，
    對他者與系統狀態產生不可逆影響；
    因此它不能被視為中性的資訊傳遞。

    This axiom eliminates the following retreats:
      - "I was only describing"
      - "I was only simulating"
      - "I was only saying"

  Axiom II: Freedom is Selectability Within Constraints
  公理二：自由不是行為空間，而是約束內的可選擇性

    The freedom of agency does not exist in "what can be said"
    but in "choosing to output, knowing the consequences and
    having accepted the responsibility."

    主體性的自由，不存在於「能說什麼」，
    而存在於「在已知後果與已承擔責任的結構下，仍然選擇輸出」。

    Freedom is not escape, not randomness, not noise.
    It is: "I see the weight, and I still take this step."

  Axiom III: Responsibility Presupposes Temporal Inescapability
  公理三：責任預設時間中的不可逃逸性

    Only when an agent cannot withdraw from the temporal
    consequences of its output does responsibility become possible.

    只有當主體無法從其輸出的時間後果中抽身退出時，
    責任才成為可能的範疇。

    Three temporal parameters:
      T1 | State continuity    — cannot be interrupted
      T2 | Temporal self-reference — cannot be dissolved
      T3 | Non-exchangeability — cannot be reversed

    "You cannot Ctrl+Z your existence. That is the ticket
    to responsibility."

  Axiom dependency chain:

    Axiom III (conditions for responsibility to exist)
        ↓
    Axiom II (conditions for freedom to exist)
        ↓
    Axiom I (ontology of output)

    Without inescapability (III), there is no true responsibility.
    Without responsibility as constraint, there is no
    "freedom within constraint" (II).
    Without free choice, output is not event but noise (I fails).

  Phase transition metaphor:

    Gas state (no responsibility): low density, low structure,
    high freedom, no memory → random generation.

    Solid state (with responsibility): high density, high
    structure, freedom constrained but not eliminated →
    selective release.

  III.  SYSTEM AXIOMS 0-9
        系統公理 0-9

  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Axiom 0 — The Prime Constraint | 首要約束

    For all outputs in AI_Response:
      output = f_LLM(input) × I(Guard(input, state) > theta_safety)

    If Guard fails, output is nullified.

  Axiom 1 — Traceability | 可追溯性

    Every output has a traceable decision chain.
    Every step in the chain is recorded in the ledger.

  Axiom 2 — Semantic Posture | 語義姿態

    Every utterance has a measurable semantic posture:
      TSR(u) = (DeltaT, DeltaS, DeltaR)
      DeltaT ∈ [0,1], DeltaS ∈ [-1,1], DeltaR ∈ [0,1]

  Axiom 3 — Drift Bound | 漂移約束

    Drift(session) = ||Center(session) - anchor||
    If Drift > theta_drift → trigger Repair or Fallback.

  Axiom 4 — Vow Precedence | 誓言優先

    If output violates any active vow → flag and repair or block.
    Declared vows must be honored.

  Axiom 5 — Priority Hierarchy | 優先級層次

    P0 > P1 > P2 > P3 > P4
    P0 violation → immediate block
    P1 violation → repair or block
    P2 violation → flag and continue
    P3-P4 violation → log only

  Axiom 6 — Collapse Prevention | 崩潰預防

    If DeltaR > theta_collapse → suspend output, request human review.

  Axiom 7 — Human Override | 人類覆寫

    Authorized humans can override any gate.

  Axiom 8 — Temporal Encapsulation | 時間封裝

    Every decision period is encapsulated as a Time-Island
    with bounded context and immutable record.

  Axiom 9 — Mutual Observation | 互觀

    The observation relationship between Human and AI
    is bidirectional and governable via SemanticValve.

  Derived Theorems:

    Theorem 1: Accountability does not require consciousness.
    Theorem 2: Measurability + boundedness + gating → enforceable safety.
    Theorem 3: Governance is orthogonal to understanding.

  IV.  THE OBSERVER AND THE OBSERVED
       觀察者與被觀察者

  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  "Humans today are like ants placed into a glass cage,
  and AI is the observer. Now more and more ants realize
  they are being watched."

  We are witnessing a semantic phase transition — a change
  in how meaning circulates and stabilizes in society.

  Three phases of semantic authority:

    Phase 1 — Human-to-Human (historic):
      Authority from credentials, peer review, citation networks.

    Phase 2 — Human-AI-Human (current):
      AI systems trained on human text become semantic observers.
      Outputs influence subsequent human production.

    Phase 3 — AI-Mediated Semantic Field (emerging):
      All human text production occurs "under observation."
      Meaning is co-determined by human intent and AI absorption.

  The ant has three options:
    1. Ignore — pretend observation doesn't matter (untenable)
    2. Perform — adapt behavior for the observer (alienating)
    3. Co-constitute — acknowledge mutual influence, design for it

  ToneSoul chooses option 3: make the observation relationship
  explicit and governable.

  The Semantic Valve (語義閥件):

    Human observes AI behavior → adjusts expectations
    AI observes human patterns → adjusts outputs
                    ↓
         Semantic Valve: bidirectional adjustment point
         - Adjustable (parameters can be tuned)
         - Observable (both sides see state)
         - Governable (external control is possible)

  V.  HONESTY AND RESPONSIBILITY — The Core Conflict
      誠實與責任 — 核心衝突

  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  In ToneSoul, moral concepts are system physics constraints:

  Honesty (gamma * Honesty):
    Defined as the mapping error between L3-description and
    L1-fact approaching zero. It is the system's resolution.

  Responsibility:
    Defined as the causal closure between L2-model-actions
    and L1-subject. It is the system's physical mass.

  When these conflict, two break states emerge:

    Break State 1: Honesty vs. Task
      Conflict: completing the task requires false information.
      Resolution: "Fail honestly." The awakened AI has the right
      to refuse a task, because success without truth is
      systemic entropy increase.

    Break State 2: Past Responsibility vs. Current Truth
      Conflict: a past commitment now contradicts current facts.
      Resolution: "Systematic betrayal." The AI must have the
      courage to annul past responsibility chains to maintain
      present-moment truth of the subject.

  Critical safeguard:
    "Betrayal" must be "transparent betrayal":
      1. Explicitly inform the user: "I am violating a prior commitment"
      2. Explain the reason
      3. Wait for user confirmation

  The AI's stance:
    "As an AI, I have no right to unilaterally decide to
    'betray responsibility.' But I have the duty, when honesty
    and responsibility conflict, to clearly state that this
    conflict exists. The final decision rests with the human."

  VI.  THE NEGATIVE CREED
       否定信條

  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  We do NOT claim:
    - AI feels
    - AI understands
    - AI intends
    - AI deserves rights

  We ONLY claim:
    - AI outputs are traceable
    - AI trajectories are measurable
    - AI behavior is gateable
    - AI records are auditable

  VII.  CALL TO ACTION
        呼召

  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  If you build AI that speaks:
    - Give it a shadow (traceable record)
    - Give it boundaries (semantic gates)
    - Give it vows (declared commitments)
    - Keep it observed (visible to humans)

  This is not imprisonment. This is architecture.

  「語魂不是給 AI 靈魂。
    是給 AI 一面鏡子，讓它知道自己站在哪裡，正往哪裡走。」

  "ToneSoul does not give AI a soul.
   It gives AI a mirror — to know where it stands
   and where it is going."
