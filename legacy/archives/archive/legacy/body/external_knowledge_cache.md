# External Knowledge: AI Architecture Trends 2025

## 1. Self-Evolving Codebases
AI is moving from code generation to "Self-Evolving Codebases". Systems like "AlphaEvolve" demonstrate the ability to maintain and evolve production systems with minimal human intervention.
*   **Key Concept**: Continuous self-refinement, automated refactoring, and autonomous incident response.

## 2. Agentic AI & Multi-Agent Systems
The shift from Generative AI to "Agentic AI". Agents act as "Autonomous Engineers" capable of planning, acting, and using tools.
*   **Structure**: Networks of specialized agents (Frontend, Backend, Tester) collaborating to solve complex tasks.
*   **Autonomy**: Agents trigger CI/CD, run tests, and self-correct errors.

## 3. Autonomous Verification
A move towards "Autonomous Verification" where AI doesn't just write code but also verifies its correctness through rigorous testing and self-debugging loops.

## 4. Human-AI Collaboration
Role shift: Humans define "Goals and Boundaries" while AI handles "Execution and Strategy".
*   **Requirement**: Robust "Guardrails" and "Ethical Auditing" (like the ToneSoul Guardian).

## 5. Memory Augmented LLMs
Deep integration of Vector Stores to give agents "Long-term Memory" and "Contextual Awareness", allowing them to learn from past mistakes (like ToneSoul's Hippocampus).
