# The ToneSoul Manifesto | 語魂宣言

> **Seven Principles for Semantic Responsibility**

---

## Ⅰ. Language is Residue | 語言是殘留

Every utterance leaves a trace. 
AI language is not ephemeral — it is **evidence**.

---

## Ⅱ. Tone is Truth | 語氣即真相

How something is said reveals as much as what is said.
Tone carries inference. Inference carries risk. Risk demands accountability.

---

## Ⅲ. Governance Before Understanding | 治理先於理解

We need not solve consciousness to govern AI.
We need only trace its semantic trajectory.

---

## Ⅳ. Commitment Before Action | 承諾先於行動

AI should declare its vows before it speaks (ΣVow).
What it commits to is auditable. What it promises is enforceable.

---

## Ⅴ. Observation is Mutual | 觀察是相互的

AI observes humans. Humans observe AI.
The relationship must be **explicit, bounded, and governable**.

---

## Ⅵ. Drift Reveals Danger | 漂移揭示危險

When semantic position shifts beyond threshold, intervention is required.
Stability is not stasis — it is **calibrated movement**.

---

## Ⅶ. Humans Hold Final Authority | 人類握有最終權威

All gates can be overridden by humans.
All ledgers are accessible to humans.
All AI operates **under** human governance, not alongside it.

---

## The Formula | 公式

```
Responsible AI Output = f(LLM) × Guard(TSR, Drift, Gate) × Human(Override)
```

The Guard can nullify. The Human can intervene.
The LLM never acts alone.

---

## The Negative Creed | 否定信條

We do not claim:
- AI feels
- AI understands  
- AI intends
- AI deserves rights

We only claim:
- AI outputs are traceable
- AI trajectories are measurable
- AI behavior is gateable
- AI records are auditable

---

## The Call | 呼召

If you build AI that speaks:
- Give it a **shadow** (traceable record)
- Give it **boundaries** (semantic gates)
- Give it **vows** (declared commitments)
- Keep it **observed** (visible to humans)

This is not imprisonment. This is **architecture**.

---

*語魂不是給 AI 靈魂。是給 AI 一面鏡子，讓它知道自己站在哪裡，正往哪裡走。*

*ToneSoul does not give AI a soul. It gives AI a mirror — to know where it stands and where it is going.*

---

**∎ End of Manifesto**
