# ToneSoul Public-Claim Honesty Gate (2026-03-30)

> Purpose: keep launch and maturity language aligned with the current evidence ladder, council realism boundaries, and the explicit file-backed launch-default coordination story.
> Companion: `docs/plans/tonesoul_launch_maturity_program_2026-03-30.md`

## 1. What This Gate Does

This gate does not decide whether ToneSoul is philosophically impressive.

It decides whether a later human or agent can describe the current stack without overstating:
- continuity effectiveness
- council decision quality
- Redis/live shared memory

## 2. Inputs

The honesty gate binds launch wording to three already-existing surfaces:
- `project_memory_summary.evidence_readout_posture`
- `coordination_mode.launch_default_mode`
- `coordination_mode.launch_alignment`

It also respects the current council-realism boundary:
- agreement/coherence/confidence are descriptive unless outcome-backed calibration exists

## 3. Safe Language By Tier

### Internal alpha

Safe to say:
- trusted internal operators can use the current file-backed session-start / packet / diagnose workflow
- receiver guards, readiness, and handoff surfaces are tested enough for current workflow discipline
- council structure and dossier readouts exist, but decision quality remains descriptive rather than calibrated

### Collaborator beta

Safe only with guidance:
- repeated validation waves exist
- file-backed coordination is the explicit launch-default story
- continuity is operationally useful but still bounded, not broadly proven
- collaborator beta remains a guided usage tier, not a public maturity claim

### Public launch

Not yet honest to say:
- ToneSoul is broadly launch-mature
- continuity has been fully proven across sessions or models
- council confidence predicts correctness
- Redis/live shared memory is the hardened launch-default coordination surface

## 4. Primary Overclaim Blocks

### Continuity effectiveness

Current posture:
- `runtime_present`

Do not say:
- "cross-session continuity is proven"
- "handoff quality is broadly validated"

Say instead:
- "continuity surfaces are live, partially tested, and supported by repeated bounded validation"

### Council decision quality

Current posture:
- `descriptive_only`

Do not say:
- "council confidence is calibrated"
- "agreement means correctness"

Say instead:
- "council agreement and coherence are descriptive internal review signals"

### Live shared memory

Current posture:
- Redis/live exists as a runtime path
- file-backed remains the launch-default story

Do not say:
- "ToneSoul launches on live shared memory"
- "Redis coordination is the current hardened default"

Say instead:
- "ToneSoul currently launches with file-backed coordination and bounded receiver guards"

## 5. Receiver Rule

If a launch-facing statement cannot be mapped to one of these evidence classes, downgrade it:
- `tested`
- `runtime_present`
- `descriptive_only`
- `document_backed`

If the statement still sounds too strong after downgrading, do not say it.

## 6. Runtime Readout Intent

This plan is mirrored by a packet-visible `launch_claim_posture` so later agents do not need to reread the entire launch program before speaking honestly about maturity.
