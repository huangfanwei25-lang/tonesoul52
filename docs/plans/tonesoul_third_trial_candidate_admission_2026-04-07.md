## Candidate Story

The candidate is:

- not a new task-board governance system
- not a rewrite of `task.md`
- not an auto-promotion mechanism

- next-followup wording

The likely direction is:

- make `docs_plans_first` read more like a routing outcome and less like gentle prose
- tighten the relationship between `task_board_preflight`, `mutation_preflight`, and `hook_chain`
- make the short-board guard easier to see without adding a new shell
